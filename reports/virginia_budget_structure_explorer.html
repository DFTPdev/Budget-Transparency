Branches show WHO controls the money based on Virginia's constitutional structure:

  • Executive (84.6%) - Programs managed by the Governor and Cabinet
  • Judicial (12.5%) - Court system and justice administration
  • Independent (2.3%) - Agencies independent from the Governor (Lottery, ABC, SCC, VRS)
  • Legislative (0.6%) - General Assembly and support agencies

üìÅ What are Secretariats?

Secretariats organize Executive Branch agencies by policy area. The Governor appoints a Secretary for each:

  • Education - Schools, universities, museums
  • Health & Human Resources - Healthcare, social services
  • Transportation - Roads, transit, DMV
  • Public Safety - Police, prisons, emergency management
  • ...and 8 more policy areas

Agencies are the actual departments and institutions that deliver services:
